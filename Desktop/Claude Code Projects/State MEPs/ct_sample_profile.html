Beatriz Gutierrez President & CEO

Meet CONNSTEP’s President & CEO:

Beatriz Gutierrez is the president and chief executive officer of CONNSTEP, Connecticuts leading consultancy firm dedicated to supporting the goals of Connecticuts small and medium-sized manufacturers.

Beatriz is an executive with over 30 years of professional experience, excelling in local and global leadership positions. Appointed President and CEO of CONNSTEP in 2020, she leads all operational aspects of the company, including the creation, implementation, and advancement of CONNSTEPs strategic plan and goals.

As the head of a leading MEP center, in the US Commerce Department, NIST network, Beatriz strives to strengthen the competitiveness and innovativeness of Connecticuts manufacturing community by championing business development, operational excellence, and technology adoption through digital transformation efforts such as Industry 4.0.

Along with her successes in new product and service development, Beatrizs expertise including business management, product marketing, and Lean methodologies, makes her uniquely qualified to lead CONNSTEP in serving the growing and evolving needs of Connecticuts thriving manufacturing industry.

Before joining CONNSTEP, Beatriz served as executive director of the Office of Business Development for Connecticuts Department of Economic and Community Development. In that role, she spearheaded several of the states business financial assistance and tax credit programs, including the Office of International Business, and the Office of Capital Projects.

Beatriz earned an MBA from the University of Chicago-Booth School of Business and a degree in electrical engineering from Marquette University. She holds Lean Six Sigma Black Belt and National Development Council Economic Development Finance Professional certifications.

Beatriz has also shared her extensive knowledge, teaching at the collegiate level, and fostering the next generation of manufacturing leaders.
